Info Tooltip

A tiny circular button with a popover for explaining metrics inline.

Fear & Greed Index A composite score from 0–100 derived from market breadth, average 24h change, and BTC dominance.

Hover the ? button on desktop. On mobile (≤640px), the parent app should switch to a bottom sheet using info_KEY_t and info_KEY_d i18n keys.

Toast

Transient notifications that auto-dismiss. Use sparingly — only for confirmations or errors a user needs to see but not act on.
